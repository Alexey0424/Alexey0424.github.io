orca-call-intelligence

Sales calls in. Emotion-aware intelligence out. Nothing leaves the machine.

ORCA is a self-hosted pipeline that turns a raw call recording into a diarized transcript, nine-class speech emotion, prosody analysis, extracted KPIs, a coached scorecard, and a branded PDF report. A pgvector index makes every call searchable, and an n8n agent lets the team chat with the whole archive.

  • 5 GPU-accelerated FastAPI services, launched with one docker compose up
  • ~95% retrieval quality at 20% of the storage, via Matryoshka-truncated embeddings
  • PII redacted with Presidio before anything reaches the database
  • Whisper
  • pyannote
  • emotion2vec
  • FastAPI
  • pgvector
  • Ollama
  • n8n
  • Docker
  • CUDA

llm-web-scraper

A plain-English prompt in. A grounded spreadsheet out. One local model.

Describe the records you want and hand over a seed URL. A local LLM plans the crawl, a best-first frontier explores the site, pages are classified by a heuristics-plus-LLM blend, and every extracted value is checked against the page text before it reaches your spreadsheet. The model is never allowed to invent data.

  • 151 tests, all running offline with no LLM required
  • Schema-constrained decoding on every single LLM call
  • Live crawl UI with plan, progress, ETA and a per-call timeline
  • Ollama
  • Flask
  • SQLite
  • Docker
  • pytest
  • JSON-LD

The event-driven nervous system a US sales team runs their day on.

Production integration suite for a real estate wholesaling company. CRM stage changes become routed Slack notifications, cold leads from a Google Form are deduplicated and assigned round-robin, and every completed call is mirrored onto the right contact with its recording, AI summary, and sentiment.
